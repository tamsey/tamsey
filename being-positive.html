master the art of being positive

Fill each day of life with colors of positivity

Posted by happiness for life on July 12, 2017

Being positive is an art

Mother nature serves us with an empty drawing sheet everyday. Everyday we get a chance to fill our life with colors of positivity, we get the chance to bring a meaningful change to our life. An opportunity to make a promise to our self to be positive for the whole day.It has never been an easy task to be positive but our efforts never stop. Lets look at one more such effort to fill our day and life with colors of positivity

Start with the orange of sunrise

Sunrise is one of the most beautiful and inspirational creations of artistic nature. But inside the beauty of it lies a reminder for all of us. Sunrise tells us that we are given yet another day, yet another reason to feel positive, a reason to start again in the hope of a better tomorrow while learning from the mistakes of yesterday

Being positive is really not about ignoring mistakes and trying to be false happy. No, nobody can ever be happy if they ignore their mistakes

Being positive is about taking the responsibility of our actions. Its about asking for forgiveness in case we perform some wrong actions by mistake. If we start being conscious about being positive, our actions start to align themselves in positive direction and our mistakes seem to be our best mentors

Glow with the bright yellow of noon

Being positive is an art and like any art, we develop it by more and more practice. We should always aim to create ourself into our own masterpiece, But before we do that we may have to create lots of smaller arts. What I mean by that is to reach the extreme brightness or the extreme heights of being positive, we usually have to go through many small acts of positivity successfully

Aim to shine like all those silver stars

And now when its night time and we have filled all the colors for the day. its the time that we submit our sheet. Its like having utmost satisfaction. Time to tell ourself that for the day we have created the best we could possibly do

Now with the dream to shine like a star, its time for some rest and to prepare for the next day where mother nature is ready to serve us yet another blank drawing sheet

Every day is a new chance to be more positive. So lets fill our lives with colors of positivity.

To go places and do things that have never been done before – that’s what living is all about.
